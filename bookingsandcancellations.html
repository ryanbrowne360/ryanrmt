Bookings:


All bookings require pre-payment at the time of reservation. Our cancellation and no show policy is strictly enforced.

Cancellations:

  • If you cancel your appointment with at least 36 hours notice, then you will be refunded 100% of the treatment cost.

  • If you cancel your appointment under 36 hours and I am able to book another patient in your reserved time, you will be refunded 100% of the treatment cost.

  • If you cancel your appointment under 36 hours and I am unable to fill the appointment timeslot with another patient, then you will be responsible for 100% of the session cost.

Life happens, however there are no exceptions to this policy. Your reserved treatment time is valueable to yourself, to your therapist, and to other patients who may have been able to use that session timeslot. If something happens that prevents you from showing up, it is not reasonable to transfer the cost of those circumstances to your therapist and their other patients. Also, by enforcing a strict cancellation policy, we are able to keep treatment costs more affordable for everyone.


Late Arrival & No Shows:


  • No shows will not be refunded.

  • Late arrivals will be given the remainder of their treatment time. Sometimes this is extended if your therapist is able to accomodate extra time.
