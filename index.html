My name is Ryan Browne. I am an RMT, nature lover, husband, and father of two. As your RMT, my purpose is to accelerate regeneration and recovery. Regeneration and recovery have both psychological and physiological components. My work addresses both.

You may wish to revive yourself from:

  • Stagnation in the mind and body - Desk warrior aches and pains, long durations of inactivity and repetitive positions, “stuck” areas resultant from immobility etc.
  • Stress in the mind and body – Overthinking, overworking, overstimulation, headaches, tension.
  • Injuries and Trauma – From minor to serious complications that impact your abilities.
  • Rigorous physical activity – Sports, Exercise, Gardening, & physically demanding occupations.

With physical manipulation of structures in your body I can create more space where resistance and compression is found. In doing so we are enhancing the flow of energy (blood, oxygen, lymphatic fluid, innervation, fuel, and nourishment) to your body’s tissues, joints, and organs.

This flow creates more space for movement and more resilience to stress, shock, and use. It will also allow for the removal of waste products such as mineral build up, damaged tissue, lactic acid, and other damaged cells the body will then transform into energy and/or expel from itself.

I cater to your individual needs and pressure tolerances. I integrate my techniques with the timing of your breathe and the beat of your heart. Like molding clay, it is possible to transform the body into an entity that functions better.
